Hi, I'm Lisa! I'm a senior at Cornell University studying Information Science with a minor in Law and Society.

In school, I serve as a Product Designer on the Cornell Design & Tech Initiative project team, which creates apps and websites for the Cornell community. On the team, I am currently designing an app called cue which will make on-campus events more discoverable for students.

In school, I have helped to teach INFO 4240: Designing Technologies for Social Impact, and I served as an undergraduate research assistant in the Communication and Collaborative Technologies Lab. I also worked as a representative for Women in Computing at Cornell (WICC), and I was both webmaster and later Director of Public Relations for the co-ed service fraternity Alpha Phi Omega. Additionally, I am an Arts and Sciences Peer Advisor, and I was a violinist in the Cornell Chamber Orchestra.

In my spare time, I enjoy exploring, volunteering, reading, and playing various instruments.
